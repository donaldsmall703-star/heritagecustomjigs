Our Story

Tied by Hand.
Proven on the Water.

Heritage Custom Jigs was born out of a passion for fishing the Outer Banks. Every jig is hand-tied and hand-painted right here in Nags Head, NC — built to perform in real conditions on real fish.

We specialize in bucktail jigs for cobia, but our custom colors work on a wide variety of inshore and offshore species up and down the coast.
